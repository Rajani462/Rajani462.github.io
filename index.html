Dr. Rajani Kumar Pradhan

Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellow
Department of Geosciences, University of Padova, Italy

Research Focus: Hydroclimatic extremes, precipitation scaling, hydrological modeling, and remote sensing.

Research

My research advances understanding of global precipitation dynamics and climate variability using satellite observations, data assimilation, and hydrological modeling.
