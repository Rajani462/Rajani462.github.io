Research

My research centers on understanding precipitation processes, extreme rainfall, and hydroclimatic variability using satellite observations, model-data integration, and statistical analysis. Key themes include:

  • Precipitation evaluation and scaling
  • Diurnal variability of rainfall
  • Hydrological impacts of extreme events
